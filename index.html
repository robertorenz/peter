Peter and the Wolf

Early one morning, Peter opened the gate and walked into the great green meadow. His friend the 🐦 bird flutters along, the 🦆 duck paddles in the pond — but a wolf is prowling. Gather every apple, then either slip home through the gate or, for a hero's ending, lure the wolf onto the rope by the old oak.

Move with WASD or the ↑←↓→ arrows. Shift to tip-toe · Space to spring the trap. Hide behind trees; the bird will harry the wolf when it gives chase.

Tip: the wolf hears you more when you run. Move softly when it's near.
Press P to pause · M to mute
